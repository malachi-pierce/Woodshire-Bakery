What Causes Climate Change?

The scientific drivers behind our warming planet

🏭

Fossil Fuels

Burning coal, oil, and gas releases CO₂ that traps heat in our atmosphere. This accounts for 76% of global greenhouse gas emissions.

+1.1°C global warming since pre-industrial times
🌳

Deforestation

Trees absorb CO₂, but we're losing 10 million hectares of forest annually. This reduces Earth's capacity to regulate carbon.

15% of global emissions from deforestation
�

Agriculture

Livestock produces methane, a potent greenhouse gas. Modern farming practices also release significant CO₂ emissions.

24% of global emissions from agriculture
🏙️

Industrial Processes

Manufacturing, construction, and chemical production release various greenhouse gases beyond CO₂.

21% of global emissions from industry

Real-World Effects

How climate change impacts our lives and ecosystems

Now

Extreme Weather

More frequent hurricanes, wildfires, droughts, and floods. 2023 saw 28 climate-related disasters costing over $1 billion each.

2030

Sea Level Rise

Ocean levels rising 3.3mm per year. Coastal cities like Miami and Venice face increased flooding and infrastructure damage.

2050

Food Security

Crop yields could decline 20% globally. 200 million people may face food insecurity due to climate impacts.

2100

Tipping Points

Potential irreversible changes including Amazon rainforest collapse and Arctic permafrost methane release.

Solutions That Work

Personal actions and policy changes that make a difference

🏠 Personal Actions

  • Reduce energy consumption at home (30% of emissions)
  • Choose sustainable transportation options
  • Adopt plant-rich diet (reduces emissions by 73%)
  • Support renewable energy providers
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle waste

🏛️ Policy Solutions

  • Carbon pricing and emissions trading
  • Investment in renewable infrastructure
  • Protected areas and reforestation programs
  • Climate education in schools
  • International climate agreements

🔬 Technological Solutions

  • Solar and wind energy expansion
  • Energy storage and grid modernization
  • Carbon capture and storage
  • Sustainable agriculture practices
  • Green hydrogen development
